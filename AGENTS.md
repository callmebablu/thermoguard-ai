# AGENTS.md

## Project

This repository contains the ThermoGuard AI marketing website.

ThermoGuard AI is a B2B industrial IoT / AI-assisted thermal-risk monitoring product concept for electrical panels, critical power environments, facilities, data centres, manufacturing sites, and maintenance operations.

The website should present ThermoGuard AI as a serious commercial product/service.

## Core positioning

ThermoGuard AI helps operations, facilities, and maintenance teams monitor electrical panel heat behaviour, detect abnormal thermal patterns earlier, receive configurable alerts, and support faster maintenance response before thermal risk contributes to avoidable downtime.

## Target audience

- Facility managers
- Electrical maintenance teams
- Plant managers
- Data centre operators
- Commercial property operators
- Safety/compliance teams
- Industrial maintenance service providers

## Marketing tone

Use a tone that is:

- Enterprise-grade
- Credible
- Technical but clear
- B2B product-focused
- Industrial IoT / predictive maintenance oriented
- Confident but not exaggerated

Avoid sounding like:

- A blog article
- A generic AI startup
- A casual consumer app
- A purely technical explainer

## Wording rules

Use wording such as:

- helps detect anomalies earlier
- supports preventive maintenance
- improves electrical asset visibility
- supports faster maintenance response
- reduces avoidable downtime
- decision-support platform
- AI-assisted risk classification
- configurable alert workflows
- integration pathway
- readiness
- designed to support

Avoid wording such as:

- guarantees safety
- prevents all fires
- certified protection
- eliminates failure
- replaces electricians
- replaces qualified inspection
- fully autonomous safety system
- industrially certified shutdown
- proven to prevent disasters

## Design direction

Preserve the current dark technical product style unless explicitly asked to redesign.

Preferred design feel:

- Premium industrial SaaS
- Industrial IoT
- AI monitoring platform
- Predictive maintenance dashboard
- Clean dark interface
- Strong cards, dashboards, grids, and workflow visuals
- Controlled use of blue, cyan, orange, red, and green for monitoring/risk states

Avoid:

- Clutter
- Too many long paragraphs
- Random stock-photo feel
- Fake customer logos
- Fake statistics
- Fake pricing
- Over-animated effects

When editing:

- Make controlled, file-specific changes.
- Do not rewrite unrelated sections.
- Do not add dependencies unless explicitly requested.
- Preserve existing styling system and animation patterns.
- Use existing components, icons, and utilities where possible.
- Run the available build/type check after implementation.
- Report changed files and build result.
