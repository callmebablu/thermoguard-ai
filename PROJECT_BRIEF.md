# ThermoGuard AI Website Brief

## What this website is

This is a commercial marketing website for ThermoGuard AI.

It should explain the business value of an AI-assisted thermal-risk monitoring and maintenance intelligence platform for electrical panels and critical power environments.

## Main message

ThermoGuard AI turns panel heat data into earlier maintenance action.

It helps teams:

- monitor electrical panel heat behaviour
- detect abnormal thermal patterns earlier
- classify risk states
- receive configurable alerts
- prioritise maintenance action
- keep event and alert history
- improve visibility across sites
- support preventive maintenance workflows

## Buyer problem

Electrical panels and critical power environments can develop heat-related risks from overload, poor ventilation, loose connections, ageing components, and operational stress.

Periodic inspections can miss issues developing between checks.

Businesses care because unexpected electrical downtime can disrupt operations, increase emergency maintenance costs, and reduce visibility across assets.

## Product value pillars

1. Earlier thermal-risk awareness
2. Faster maintenance response
3. Reduced avoidable downtime
4. Better asset visibility and reporting evidence
5. Maintenance prioritisation
6. Multi-site monitoring potential

## AI value

AI should be presented as practical business intelligence, not as vague hype.

AI helps with:

- anomaly detection
- risk scoring
- trend analysis
- alert prioritisation
- recurring pattern detection
- maintenance recommendations
- reporting intelligence

## Integration story

ThermoGuard AI should be presented as fitting into maintenance and operations workflows.

Potential integration areas:

- Email/SMS alerts
- Team notifications
- Maintenance logs
- CMMS/ticketing readiness
- BMS/SCADA pathway
- API-ready workflows
- Reporting exports
- Multi-site dashboard visibility

Use cautious language such as “readiness”, “pathway”, “designed to support”, and “future workflow” unless the integration is actually implemented.

## Plans strategy

Do not show fixed public prices.

Use demo-led B2B plan cards:

### Starter
For teams beginning with critical panel monitoring.

### Professional
For multi-panel and multi-site operations.

### Enterprise
For custom deployment, integrations, advanced analytics, and support.

Preferred CTAs:

## Trust statement

Use this idea somewhere subtle:

ThermoGuard AI is designed as a monitoring and decision-support platform for maintenance visibility. It supports professional inspection and maintenance workflows; it does not replace certified electrical safety systems or qualified electrical inspection.
